Use of the app

Piwigo Media for Canva is provided to connect Canva with a Piwigo instance controlled by the user. Users are responsible for installing and maintaining the Piwigo Connector plugin on their own Piwigo instance.

User responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the rights to access, use, modify, and upload the media they manage through the app.

Connector tokens

Connector tokens grant access to albums, selected photo files, and upload actions on the connected Piwigo instance. Users should keep tokens private and revoke tokens that are no longer needed.

Availability

The app depends on Canva, the user's browser, and the user's Piwigo instance. Availability may vary based on those systems.
