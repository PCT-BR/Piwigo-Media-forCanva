Piwigo Media for Canva

Connect a self-hosted Piwigo photo library to Canva without routing photos or Piwigo credentials through a central service.

Privacy Policy Terms Support Connector plugin

How connection works

  1. Install the open-source Canva Connector plugin in your Piwigo instance.
  2. Open the connector page from your Piwigo admin account.
  3. Authorize Canva access after reviewing the listed permissions.
  4. Copy the generated connector token.
  5. Paste the token into the Canva app.

The connector token is generated by your Piwigo instance and can be revoked from your Piwigo admin page.
